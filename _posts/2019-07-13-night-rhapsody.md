Vocal holidays gives breath anew to vices  
Inner circles long swayed our comfort  
No calendar fixtures were penned, no calls  
We left behind the contemporary weights.  


We met, us, audacious nobles of gatherings  
Inhaled quadrants of talkative billow smoke  
And exhaled cocktails of tobacco, eased sighs  
Alcohol exhumes its clear disarming fragrance.

Dinner hailed ripe opportune night vacancies  
Musical selections of nostalgia allotted  
Life's coy were all lyricized and composed  
Laughing with us, passion's fool the glory.  

God's night ended as it began, with ourselves  
Unspoked adulations held silk exotic curves  
Sensuous fine viscosity thrusted, appreciated  
Neither currency nor time was felt to be paid.

I missed a train scheduled  
To depart an earshot of goodbyes  
I let it be missed, so I can say  
By how much I listened to the tracks  
Clicking the rustling payloads  
Paid and checked with a number  

Maybe I don't want a ticket?  
Expecting an abrupt stop, ruined day  
I should know which taxes it goes to.  

Maybe there is a saying:  
Kept warm underneath a jacket,  
Pull out a paper's joint,  
Want to be fully revealed,  
Tender dinner selection,  
Worth the wait, check's cleared,  
I don't need to come back,  
But the service was good.

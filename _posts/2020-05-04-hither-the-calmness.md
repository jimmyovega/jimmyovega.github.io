Wilted is a sun full of compost  
To consume the worms don't ask  
Bottom dwelling  
Renew the earth  
Stain the earth  
Erode the earth  
Make the Earth  
Quickly immolate the dissolved  
Quarter filled end product  
Slithering mulchers moon-awakened  
Croaking the emulsion pulse  
Regurgitating dirt replenishes  
Softness that rises from the bottom  
Upturned the foliage scenery  
Take it back downwards and turn  
To a pitch black serenade commune.
